# Globex — Client Brief

Single source of truth for Globex. Every specialist reads this before doing
Globex work. Keep it current.

## Snapshot
- What they do: enterprise data-compliance platform (SOC2, GDPR, audit prep).
- Stage: profitable, ~200 people, established.
- My engagement: fractional growth advisor, 1 day/week, since Mar 2026.
- Main contact: Priya Nair, VP Marketing.

## Goals (this quarter)
- Primary: generate 30 qualified demo requests/month from organic + content.
- Secondary: sharpen positioning against two named competitors.

## Audience
- Buyer: VP Eng, CISO, or Head of Compliance at a 200-2000 person company.
- Technical and risk-averse. Reads carefully. Hates fluff and hype.
- Cares about: passing audits without burning the team, defensible evidence,
  not getting fired when something goes wrong.

## Voice and tone
- Precise, credible, calm. Senior-to-senior, never salesy.
- Specifics and proof over adjectives. Cite standards by name.
- It's fine to be technical. This audience respects depth.

## Banned words and rules
- No em dashes.
- Banned: effortless, magic, "set it and forget it" (compliance is never that).
- Every claim about coverage or certification must be exact and sourced.

## Constraints
- All public claims reviewed by Globex legal. Build in lead time.
- Comparison content must be factual and fair. No competitor bashing.

## Useful context
- Their edge: fastest audit-evidence collection in the category, strong support.
- Their weak spot: pricing is opaque. Don't promise pricing details we can't back.
